To bump your stats and attributes in Arydia, you must perform the **Level Up action at the Exiles' Guild** by spending your accumulated Experience Points (XP). 

Unlike many roleplaying games where stats increase incrementally or left-to-right across your card, Arydia uses a unique column-based system. Each time you gain a new level, you resolve the corresponding level column on the back of your Character card from top to bottom:
1. **Spend the XP cost** required for that specific level.
2. **Mark the level box** you are gaining.
3. **Mark one box** in either **HP, MP, or SP** (your stats).
4. **Mark one box** in either **STR, DEX, INT, WIS, CHA, or END** (your attributes).

Your chosen stat and attribute are **immediately set to the new value listed in that level's column**. The stats and attributes you do *not* select remain at whatever values they were previously set to. 

---

### Step-by-Step Example: Levelling Up from Level 3 to Level 4

Let us use a **Human character** to demonstrate how this works. Here is a look at the Human level-up progression chart from the rulebook:

| LVL (Level) | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | **4** |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **XP Cost** | — | 3 | 5 | 7 | **10** |
| **HP** (Hit Points) | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | **8** |
| **MP** (Mana Points) | 2 | 3 | 4 | 4 | **5** |
| **SP** (Stamina Points) | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | **4** |
| **Attributes** (all STR, DEX, etc.) | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | **5** |

#### Your Starting State (Level 3)
Imagine your character is currently Level 3. Over your previous level-ups, you chose HP at Level 3, MP at Level 2, and INT at Level 2. You have never chosen SP or STR, meaning they are still at their starting values. Your sheet currently looks like this:
*   **Max HP:** 7 *(chosen at Level 3)*
*   **Max MP:** 4 *(chosen at Level 2)*
*   **Max SP:** 1 *(never chosen, remains at starting Level 0 value)*
*   **STR:** 1 *(never chosen, starting value)*
*   **INT:** 3 *(chosen at Level 2)*

#### 1. Pay the XP Cost
To level up from Level 3 to Level 4, you must spend **10 XP** from your character mat by returning it to the supply. You then mark the Level 4 box on your character card.

#### 2. Bump Your Stat (HP, MP, or SP)
You decide your character desperately needs more physical energy to travel, so you choose to mark the **SP box** in the Level 4 column.
*   **The Bump:** Because you chose SP at Level 4, your **MAX SP immediately jumps from 1 to 4**.
*   **Replenishing:** You adjust your MAX SP token on your player mat to 4, and then **fully replenish your HP, MP, and SP to their max values**.
*   **What about HP and MP?** Because you did not choose HP or MP at Level 4, they remain at their previous values (Max HP is still 7, Max MP is still 4). 

#### 3. Bump Your Attribute (STR, DEX, INT, WIS, CHA, or END)
You want your exile to strike harder in combat, so you choose to mark the **STR box** in the Level 4 column.
*   **The Bump:** Your **STR immediately jumps from 1 to 5**. You adjust your physical Strength token on your player mat to match.
*   **What about other attributes?** Your other attributes remain completely unchanged at their previous values.

By the end of this process, your character has successfully transitioned to Level 4, boasting a newly maximised **SP of 4** and a mighty **STR of 5**.
